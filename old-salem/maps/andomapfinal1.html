Key to Symbols and Numbers

The number before a name or group of names indicates location of the house on the map.

Note that it was common for different families to occupy the same residence.

A number after a name (1, 2 or 3) indicates generation.

An asterix * before a name indictes involvement in the witchcraft trials.
On the map, these residences are marked with a black roof.
